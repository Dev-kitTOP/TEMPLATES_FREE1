¿Cómo reciben donaciones las organizaciones sin fines de lucro?
Esta organización sin fines de lucro recibirá pagos de Facebook cada dos semanas cuando recaude al menos $100 en donaciones.

¿Qué impuestos se aplican?
Las donaciones a organizaciones sin fines de lucro de los Estados Unidos de tipo 501(c)(3) suelen ser deducibles de impuestos. En cambio, las donaciones a recaudaciones de fondos personales normalmente no lo son.
Consulta a un asesor para informarte si las donaciones son deducibles de impuestos.

This will be my 10th straight year riding in the Pan Mass Challenge and raising funds to fight the scourge that is cancer. I am more committed than ever to this cause.

So far I have raised $118,000+ for DFCI.

I am a proud supporter of the PMC because it is a cause that everyone can get behind. 100% of rider-raised revenue goes directly to support the Jimmy Fund and Dana-Farber Cancer Institute's cancer fighting efforts. Please help me to reach my goal and in turn help the PMC to reach its overall goal by donating to this worthwhile cause. Every dollar that you give helps, so do not feel that a small contribution would be insignificant. Every dollar raised brings us a dollar closer to a cure. Thanks for your support!

Jeff

Pan-Mass Challenge
Organización sin fines de lucro 501(c)(3) de los EE. UU.
The mission of the Pan-Mass Challenge is to raise money for life-saving cancer care and research at Dana-Farber ...Cancer Institute through its Jimmy Fund. The catalyst for the fundraising effort is a bike-a-thon that crosses the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. Committed cyclists, volunteers, corporate sponsors, and individual financial contributors are essential to the PMC achieving this goal. Since its founding in 1980, the PMC has been increasingly successful in mobilizing the support of all these constituencies. We strive to inspire even more proponents of our cause and intend the money we raise to afford Dana-Farber's doctors and researchers the resources necessary to discover cures for all cancers. Ver más
