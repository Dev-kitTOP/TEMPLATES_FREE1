¿Quién puede hacer donaciones a esta recaudación de fondos?
En este momento, solo las personas de algunos países pueden hacer donaciones a recaudaciones de fondos en Facebook. Haz clic para ver el artículo con la lista completa de países.

triangle-right
¿Cómo reciben donaciones las organizaciones sin fines de lucro?
Las donaciones hechas a esta recaudación de fondos se pagarán directamente a la organización sin fines de lucro mediante su procesador de pagos.
Ver artículo completo
triangle-right
¿Qué impuestos se aplican?
Las donaciones a organizaciones sin fines de lucro de los Estados Unidos de tipo 501(c)(3) suelen ser deducibles de impuestos.
Consulta a un asesor para informarte si las donaciones son deducibles de impuestos.
Ver artículo completo
triangle-right
¿Hay cargos?
Facebook no cobra comisiones por las donaciones a organizaciones sin fines de lucro. Algunas organizaciones pagan comisiones a terceros que ayudan a facilitar sus donaciones online.

Team Quantum has been walking in the CF Great Strides Walk since 1989 when my son Danny was, four years old. Danny, born with Cystic Fibrosis has been fighting an incredible battle for 35 years. Today married with a new one year old daughter, Shannon, and dedicated wife he is recovering from for his second double lung transplant.

It is amazing that when he was born, the doctors held little hope for Danny to survive into his teens. We raised him to believe a full life was possible and he has accomplished so much and reached milestones that were thought to be not possible... graduated college, captained his ice hockey team, married an beautiful, amazing woman and has one adorable baby girl!!!! Thank you to all of my Quantum teammates, friends and family for always being there to support us. We would not have done this withput you.

For too long, cystic fibrosis, a rare, genetic disease, has robbed people of tomorrows - progressively limiting th...eir ability to breathe and tragically shortening life. There are approximately 30,000 Americans living with CF. They are moms, dads, sisters, brothers, daughters, sons, friends, and co-workers who struggle every day in the face of this devasting disease.

We walk for Danny and all of the CF children and young adults.. Will you join me and support my Great Strides fundraising goal?
